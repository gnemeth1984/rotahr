## Strategy Notes

### Audience Segments
| Segment | Angle |
|---|---|
| Bar/restaurant owners | Rota chaos, cover scrambles, WhatsApp madness |
| Hotel managers | Multi-shift complexity, seasonal staff, compliance |
| Cafe owners | Small team, "too small" objection, simplicity |
| Multi-venue operators | Centralised control, per-venue reporting |
| HR managers | Labour cost visibility, per-employee data, compliance |

### Timing
- Day 1: Hook with pain
- Day 3: Quantify the cost
- Day 7: Social proof
- Day 14: Feature depth (AI/bookkeeping)
- Day 21: Crush objections
- Day 30: Final nudge, personal tone

### Channel Notes
- **Cold outbound:** Use Email 1 only. Warm up domain first (10–20 sends/day, ramp over 2 weeks)
- **Lead magnet follow-up:** Start at Email 2 (they already know who you are)
- **Post-trial:** Separate win-back sequence needed
- **Sender name:** Use "Gabor, Rotahr" not just "Rotahr" — personal name gets better open rates in cold outreach

### Tools (free tier)
- **Brevo** (formerly Sendinblue) — free up to 300 emails/day, sequence automation
- **Apollo.io** — free tier for finding hospitality manager contacts in Ireland
- **Hunter.io** — verify email addresses before sending
